Ark

date 04 December 2011
category Public
location Brzeg, Poland
phase Concept
authors Dawid Huczyński,

Diploma project. It is and revitalisation of an old decaying church. I worked close with all the historical documents, as well as previous projects. „The higher the church ceiling the closer to god”. The idea was to push the audience into the upper level of the interior. Ground floor instead was arranged as the exposition and foyer.
